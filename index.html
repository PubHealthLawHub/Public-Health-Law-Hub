Mission Statement

Our mission is to connect organizations with public health law and policy experts to design, manage, and complete legal epidemiology studies. We aim to fill research gaps caused by underfunding, restrictions, and other barriers, advancing evidence-based solutions for healthier communities.

Our Services

Legal Epidemiology Assessments

We study the impact of laws and interventions on health outcomes, identifying inequities and informing evidence-based solutions.

Policy Analysis

We evaluate policy options and recommend evidence-based approaches that improve effectiveness, efficiency, and equity.

Legal Research

We analyze laws, regulations, and court decisions to answer questions, support arguments, and strengthen evidence-based action.
