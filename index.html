The Public Health Law Hub is an innovative initiative led by experienced public health attorneys. We connect legal epidemiologists with project partners to provide accurate and reliable research that protects and improves population health.

About Us

The Hub is a collective of public health law and policy experts offering consulting services such as legal epidemiology assessments, legal research, and policy analysis. We match the right experts to each project, ensuring high-quality results without the need for permanent staff.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect organizations with public health law and policy experts to design, manage, and complete legal epidemiology studies—filling gaps caused by underfunding and research restrictions.

Services

Legal Epidemiology Assessments

Study the impact of laws on public health using systematic methods to identify health inequities and inform evidence-based solutions.

Policy Analysis

Evaluate and recommend policies with evidence-based insights to improve effectiveness, efficiency, and equity in outcomes.

Legal Research

Identify and analyze laws, regulations, and court decisions to answer critical public health legal questions.
